Yoga Training

The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj," which means to yoke or unite. In the context of yoga practice, it refers to the union of the mind, body, and spirit. Yoga is a holistic practice that aims to create balance and harmony within the individual and with the world around them.

Cardio Training

Cardio Yoga is a type of yoga class that combines Yoga moves with cardiovascular exercises. The workout takes classic yoga moves and makes them a bit faster, with a more rapid flow between sequences. The non-stop movement gets your heart beating and your muscles burning.
